点菜：
take your order 点菜--主语是waiter，不是顾客
	May I take your order, Sir? 先生，你点菜吗？
	
先生，我是您的服务员今晚 I'll be your waiter this evening, Sir.
你是否再需要些时间呢 Do you need a few more minutes?
我想要.... I'd like to have .../I'd like ...
		I'd like a steak, well done, and a side order of corn. And a garden salad.
		I'd like the roast chicken.
		I'd like a glass of tea.
before your meal 饭前--比如饭前是否来个开胃菜？
你想喝点什么么？ Do you have anything to drink?
你喜欢咖啡还是茶？ Do you prefer coffee or tea?
你吃什么点心？ Can I get you any dessert?
你还想要点什么么? Would you like anything else?
就这些了吗？ Will that be all?
我很快会把烤鸡和玉米带过来。 I'll be back in a minute with your roast chicken and corn.
pass on 不要什么
	 I'll pass on the soup. 我不要汤
