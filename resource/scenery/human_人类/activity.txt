	take turns 轮流
	take turns giving her leg a daily massage 轮流给她的腿每日按摩一次

	bring sb sth 带给某人某物


look:	
	look for sth/sb寻找
		look for a english textbook 寻找一本英文教科书
		look for a missing girl 寻找一个丢失的女孩
		look for a pet dog 寻找一只宠物犬
	look through sth 仔细检查，审查(某物)
		look through the package/the CDs 仔细检查了包裹/CDs
	look like + 从句 看起来象....
		It looks like he is out. 看起来象他不在家
		It looks like he is ill. 看起来象他病了
		It looks like he will lose the race. 看起来象他要输掉这场比赛了。
